S.S.R Sushant Singh Rajput


Sushant Singh Rajput, an accomplished actor known for his roles in films like "Kai Po Che!", "MS Dhoni: The Untold Story", and "Chhichhore", was admired not only for his acting talent but also for his intellectual curiosity and philosophical outlook.

About the legend

☛ Born on January 21, 1986, in Patna, Bihar, India.

☛ Graduated from Delhi Technological University (DTU) with a degree in Mechanical Engineering.

☛ Started his career with television serials like "Kis Desh Mein Hai Meraa Dil" and "Pavitra Rishta".

☛ Made his Bollywood debut with "Kai Po Che!" in 2013, gaining critical acclaim.

☛ Portrayed Indian cricket captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni in the biopic "MS Dhoni: The Untold Story" (2016).

☛ Known for his versatile acting and dedication to his craft.

☛ Actively involved in philanthropy, supporting education for underprivileged children.

☛ Had a keen interest in astronomy and science.

☛ Founded a company dedicated to virtual reality and artificial intelligence.

☛ Advocated for mental health awareness and education.

☛ Starred in movies like "Shuddh Desi Romance" (2013), "Detective Byomkesh Bakshy!" (2015), and "Raabta" (2017).

☛ Hosted a few episodes of the reality show "Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa".

☛ Believed in the importance of pursuing one's passions and dreams.

☛ Participated in activities promoting fitness and outdoor sports.

☛ His sudden demise in June 2020 shocked fans and the film industry.

☛ Remembered for his infectious smile, humility, and love for learning.

☛ Continues to inspire through his films, philanthropy, and legacy in Indian cinema.
